Image Macros

These are the most traditional type of memes, featuring an image with overlaid text, often in the form of a caption or speech bubbles. Examples include the "Advice Animals" series like "Success Kid" or "Grumpy Cat.

Reaction Memes

These memes express emotions or reactions to specific situations, often using images or GIFs from movies, TV shows, or viral videos. They are used to convey feelings such as laughter, surprise, or frustration.

Relatable Memes

These memes depict everyday situations or experiences that many people can relate to, often with humor or irony. They capitalize on shared experiences to create a sense of camaraderie among viewers.

Wholesome Memes

Contrary to dank memes, wholesome memes focus on positivity, kindness, and uplifting messages. They aim to spread joy and encouragement among viewers.

Niche Memes

These memes cater to specific communities, interests, or fandoms, using inside jokes, references, or terminology that may only be understood by a particular group of people.

Video Memes

These memes consist of short video clips, often edited or remixed for humorous effect. They can range from Vine compilations to TikTok trends and are widely shared on social media platforms.

Dank Memes

Dank memes are characterized by absurd or surreal humor, often featuring bizarre images or nonsensical text. They thrive on irony, randomness, and internet subculture references.
